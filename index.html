Rice Lake Plains Natural Area

Explore headwater streams in the Rice Lake Plains.

Project Background

The goal of this project was to create a web app showing streams in the Rice Lake Plains - Lake Iroquois Plains Natural Area and Strahler stream order to identify headwater streams. Headwater streams are defined as stream orders 1 or 2. As the client, the Nature Conservancy of Canada, uses Esri technology the team decided that an application configured with Esri commercial-off-the-shelf software was the ideal route to take. Different software options were tried and our experiences with each is documented in the "ArcGIS Solutions" page.

Problem Statement

Ideal

A web app that provides stream order and background about the Rice Lake Plains - Lake Iroquois Plains Natural Area.

Reality

Rice Lake Plains - Lake Iroquois Plains Natural Area has no mapped headwater streams.

Consequences

Without headwater stream mapping, it is difficult to identify and prioritize areas for protections.

Proposal

Create a web app showing streams geographically bounded to the Rice Lake Plains - Lake Iroquois Plains Natural Area and Strahler stream order information.

Technology Used

ArcGIS Experience Builder, ArcGIS Hub, ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Online
